INTRODUCTION

Okeke med equip is a company specialized in the sale of new and reconditioned medical equipment. We help you throughout the realization of your project according to your needs. From inception, we have been established as a leading medical supplies company in the world through outstanding reliability and customer service delivery. Our competencies have got us exclusive distribution rights to some medical brands in the world. This privilege positions us to negotiate extremely competitive prices with our suppliers enabling you to enjoy a quality product at the best possible price.
